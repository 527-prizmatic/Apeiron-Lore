Gangs & Criminality

Apeiron's Society

Every big city, no matter how rich or good to live in, has a bit of a dark side; some criminal organizations, gangs, notable criminals, or corrupt politicians. In a world ruled almost exclusively by the big fishes, everyone else is considered prone to fall into the other side of the law. As the campaign will almost exclusively take place in Infinium City, only the latter's gangs will be listed in this series of articles.
