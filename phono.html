Name Pronounciation Guide

General Info

Many important names within Apeiron come from other languages, most commonly Planetary, and as such their exact phonetics can often differ from English's pronunciation. As some names might be quite tough to pronounce, this guide will list most names that have an exotic pronunciation, as well as a rough English equivalent of their actual pronunciation. You can hover above each entry to find a narrow IPA transcription.

  • K-e'anova - [hey-'AH-naw-wa]
  • Apeiron - [ah-way-'EE-lon] (Planetary-style) or [ah-PAY-ron] (English-style)
  • Infinium (as in Infinium City) - [in-FEE-nyoom]
  • Zandara-4 - [JAAN-da-la JAY-ree] (note: the last R is rolled)
  • Zandara-5 - [JAAN-da-la kell-LU-lee] or simply [JAN-da-la]
  • Volnexar - [wll-NAY-ha] (Planetary-style) or [vall-NECK-sir] (English-style)
  • Halkeiro - [shall-KEY-law]
  • AZN - [AH-jaa-nah] (Planetary-style, acronym)
  • K-eimi Rekāri (the Planetary language) - [HAY-me lay-KAA-lee]
