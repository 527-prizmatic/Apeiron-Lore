For the purposes of worldbuilding, the Planetary language has been fully created and fleshed out, and is now practically complete. It disposes of a grammar relatively similar to English, a mostly a priori vocabulary sprinkled with loanwords from mainly Greek and Latin, and a relatively complex phonology.
