Apeiron's Main Cities

Geography & Topology

Approximately 80% of Apeiron's 127 million inhabitants are concentrated in its four main cities, while the rest is scattered around the island, into small cities and residential complexes located here and there along the island's transcontinental highways. This series of articles will mainly focus around the federation's four major cities, along with the Supernova.
