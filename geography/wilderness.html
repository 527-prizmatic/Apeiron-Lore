The Great Wilderness

Geography & Topology

Outside from Apeiron's four main cities, the island's population is sparsely scattered in multiple small cities and residential complexes. Beyond the inhabited regions, Apeiron is an endless expanse of polar wilderness, ruled solely by diastrophin-empowered wildlife. This series of articles will provide a basic overview of Apeiron's most notable geographical landmarks.

Not much is known about what Neo-Caryonite used to look like before the arrival of man in the wake of the Post-Heliostat Era. Lying between the 77th and 82th northern parallels, the landmass's extreme proximity to the North Pole makes it a region of extremely cold climates, eternally covered in ice and where only local wildlife was able to thrive.
