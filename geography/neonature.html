Neo-Nature

Geography & Topology

The term of neo-nature is an extremely broad one, but generally designates the state of an ecosystem after experiencing any sort of large-scale human alteration. This may include anything ranging from introducing new species, to razing a forest for any reason, and anything in-between. One of the most iconic alternations done to K-e'anova's biosphere is probably the massive release of diastrophin in the wild.

This series of articles will focus on the most unusual aspects of Neo-Caryonite's new environment.
