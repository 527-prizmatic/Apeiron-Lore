Timeline Summary

Apeiron's History

This page gathers a recap of the major events along Apeiron's timeline for quick reference.

  • 1848 EA to 1871 EA - Construction of the OSS-Heliostat
  • 1880 EA - Attempts at mitigating the Heliostat's effects
  • 1888 EA - Creation of diastrophin
  • 1935 EA - Official foundation of Zandara-4
  • 1948 EA / 0 PC - Poorly controlled destruction of the OSS-Heliostat. 37 megacities annihilated
  • 2 PC - Discovery of MDS-5. Public health crisis declared. Shutdown of all diastrophin plants worldwide
  • 9 PC - Aurasphere Corp. founds Terminal One
  • 10 PC - First spaceship launched since the Heliostat cataclysm. Official foundation of Aurora Prime
  • 29 PC to 57 PC - Reconstruction of Terminal One
  • 41 PC - Foundation of Terminal Two
  • 49 PC - First draft of the Planetary language
  • 53 PC - Second draft of the Planetary language
  • 71 PC - Planetary language officially adopted as business language worldwide
  • 104 PC to 110 PC - Construction of the Gate
  • 108 PC - Fusion of Terminal One and Two; official foundation of Infinium City
  • 110 PC - Aurora Prime officially renamed to Starcaller Prime
  • 139 PC - MDS Invasion
  • 142 PC - Creation of a military base on Neo-Caryonite's east coast
  • 146 PC - Official foundation of Volnexar
  • 147 PC - Starcaller Prime, Zandara-4 and Infinium City officially merge into the Apeiron Federation
  • 148 PC - Volnexar joins the Apeiron Federation
  • 153 PC - Creation of the VMS in Volnexar
  • 202 PC to 221 PC - Construction of the Supernova
  • 248 PC - Steelbeast Incident
  • 285 PC - Present day
