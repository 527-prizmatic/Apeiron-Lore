The Post-Heliostat Era

Apeiron's History

The Post-Heliostat Era refers to the second and current main era of modern history, coming right after the Era of Awakening, and whose starting point is commonly agreed as being the beginning of the bombardments of the OSS-Heliostat. It is a period of slow but steady regeneration and renewal, but is marked by stronger-than-ever and tensions between the different social classes, caused by a worldwide compulsion to rebuild the world as fast as possible with little to no regard for quality of life.
