The Era of Awakening

Apeiron's History

The Era of Awakening is the name given to the era anterior to the OSS-Heliostat's downfall, dubbed as such as it is a period of great technological progress, the "awakening" of humanity's now limitless power, their evolution from spears and stones to guns and computers, and the devastating consequences on the ecosystem this evolution gave rise to. Apeiron is set in the far future, and its timeline associates the advancement of our real world's society to roughly the year 1800 EA. Based on this model, Apeiron would be set in the early 27th century.
