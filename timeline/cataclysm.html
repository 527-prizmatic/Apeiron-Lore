The Heliostat Cataclysm

Apeiron's History

The Heliostat Cataclysm is a chain of events which marks a smooth transition between the Era of Awakening and the Post-Heliostat Era. A time of widespread doubt and uncertainty over the world's future, it is mostly known for the Heliostat's downfall and a worldwide public health emergency, but in fact includes everything since the beginning of the Heliostat-induced ice age, up until the creation of the Planetary language.
