Apeiron

The complete setting documentation
for your next cyberpunk adventure

The year is 285 PC. After two millenia of constant technological advancement and rampant industrialization, humanity's hubris led them to unthinkable actions against their own planet, in an ever-growing desire to control the uncontrollable. After three centuries of climatic cataclysms, self-inflicted disasters and crises, and two close calls at complete extinction, man is now actively seeking for a more hospitable planet, where, they hope, they will not commit the same mistakes as before.

Among the few territories leading this global effort, the mighty Federation of Far North Technopoleis, colloquially known as Apeiron, spanning over the entirety of the island of Neo-Caryonite, has no equal in terms of technological advancement, total stranglehold over the planet's economy, and socio-economic corporate diktat. Led by a government corrupted to the core, not-so-secretly working hand-in-hand with the planet's mightiest corporations, the only thing driving forward society as a whole is profit, and whoever is unable to contribute is eliminated to make way for those who can. In a world where only the rich and influent ones can thrive, what can the rest of the population possibly do to assert their own rights?

This document intends to gather all the knowledge, history and lore one needs to know before delving in an adventure within the setting of Apeiron. Simply look at the table of contents on the right and pick what you'd like to read about. Happy reading!
