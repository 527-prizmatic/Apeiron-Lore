Technological Breakthroughs

Apeiron's Economy

There's no stopping progress, as they say. Even three worldwide crises in one century and all the subsequent events following the Heliostat's destruction could merely slow it down temporarily, but pushed ever forward by the planet's largest corporations, research and development has made a series of breakthroughs which eventually brought about a massive revolution in day-to-day life.

Giving an integral breakdown would take too long, so this series of articles will provide a quick overview of the most notable and omnipresent creations of modern science. Other fields of advancements will not be mentioned, either because they are not of great interest or because they are obvious (yes, I don't think I need to tell you that computers have evolved a lot since back then).
